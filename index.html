Session 1 - 25-02-2025

Player overzicht:

Name Character Species Class - Subclass
Keanu Kaelion Sylren Wood Elf Druid - Circle of the Stars

Kris Sabor Aasimar Warlock

Jovan Arnihl Goliath Monk -

We start our adventure in a tavern, The Flaming Skull in Olostin's Hold, a tavern named for the flaming skull in the middle of the tavern.
It's a busy tavern, where Kaelion, Sariel, Buttercup and Arnihl all share a table, they've known each other for a few months now, travelling on the roads together.
With it being a busy night, strangers are bound to be sat together, as is the deal with Sabor, an Aasimar, who joins our table. A table full of well-traveled people that now looks a lot like a group of adventurers.

As a bartender takes our order she looks around the table and sees us - A party of adventurers (Party):

  • Arnihl: A 2.2m tall Goliath with gray skin, no hair and completely black eyes drinking his problems away - hails from the Spine of the world
  • Buttercup: A 1.2m tall gnome, dressed in leaf-like clothes, all earthly color, with a long braid of dark brown hair
  • Sabor: An Aasimar with blue metallic skin, black spiky hair, dressed in black/red leathers. Kind of like a tiefling without horns, but with a tail - hails from Triboar
  • Sariel: A High Elf dressed in green colours, long green hair bound in a ponytail
  • Kaelion: A Wood Elf dressed quite nicely with long red hair braided on the side of their head, but in a loose ponytail

We decide to get some rooms for the night, and ask the barmaid what the price of that would be. A simple room costs 5sp per room per night.
The barmaid also mentions that The Lady of the Hold would have work for adventurers, and if we're interested we should talk to her

We share a conversation about who we are and what we do, Arnihl likes to punch people with his fists, Sariel and Kaelion keep mysterious by saying they "dabble", Sabor makes a sword magically appear, with a green flash and smoke that smells a lot like an old, ancient forest. Buttercup is good at shooting people and generally sneaking around.
Buttercup, out of nowhere, asks everyone if they have scars, Arnihl mentions he has too many to count, Sabor says he's too prett to have scars, Sariel has some scratches here and there, the unavoidable ones. Kaelion mentions he has a large scar across his back that they might someday show to Buttercup.

In the middle of this conversation, a female human walks in, broad shoulders, long black curly hair. She almost walks past our table, but doubles back and introduces herself as Tamalin, The Lady of the Hold. There's trouble around the hold, now that the giant issues appear to be solved. There are werewolves plaguing the hold and the surrounding area. There are men-wolf shapeshifters causing trouble roughly 5-6 miles west of Olostin's Hold. If we manage to clear out the issue and bring her proof of this (preferably in the form of heads) we will be awarded 100gp for the group. The attacks happen mostly at midnight, and are not bound to the stance of the moon. The only other piece of advice Tamalin gives us is; Don't get bitten by the werewolves.

After a long rest in the tavern we get to the hall where Tamalin has kindly given us backpacks with basic supplies; bedroll, torches, rations, and the likes.

After a hearty breakfast we head on out, by foot, to the west. Roughly 5-6 miles away from Olostin's Hold we stumble upon a deserted wagon, covered in claw marks, no bodies around. appears to be a group of 5-6 people.

Thankfully the attackers (most likely the werewolves) left tracks that Kaelion manages to follow into the woods.
Marching order walking through the woods: Kaelion - Arnihl - Buttercup - Sariel - Sabor
